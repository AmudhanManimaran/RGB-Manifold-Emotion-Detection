🎨 HSI & Emotion Reference Guide

Based on Plutchik's Wheel of Emotions

📊 What is HSI?

HSI stands for Hue, Saturation, and Intensity - a color model that represents colors in a way that's more intuitive to human perception than RGB.

🎨 Why HSI Matters

Understanding HSI helps in emotion detection because different hues, saturation levels, and intensities evoke specific emotional responses in viewers.

🔬 Our Approach

We analyze artwork by extracting HSI values and mapping them to Plutchik's emotional color associations for accurate emotion detection.
